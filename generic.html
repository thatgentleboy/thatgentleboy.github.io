LEARNINGS AT AMAZON

I started working for Amazon as an SDS Cap Associate in July 2019. In this role, I contributed to raising the bar of customer service by redefining service levels and giving accurate and quick responses to the end-users’ queries and concerns. After April 2020, I was promoted to the capacity of SWAT Associate and guiding the other teams in resolving customer issues and ensuring a high service benchmark.

I've been acknowledged for my ability to address difficult problems autonomously and effectively. Until recently, I had a 97 per cent overall happiness rating on Amazon. In a short period of time, I've also progressed to becoming an SME. These roles are a wonderful fit for my skill set. I'm able to find my place in a group and contribute to everyone's efforts.

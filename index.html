Lifelong learning is something that I am very passionate about. Growing up when I looked around, I often didn’t see many options available. It wasn't until I started getting serious about my education that I realized I had the control to make my own opportunities with education being the key that would open those doors.

I always had a curiosity to disassemble the home appliances and robotic toys to check their issues. While I managed to fix some devices back, and some had to be attended by experts, this practice fixed my association with mechanical engineering, leading me to pursue my bachelor’s in it. The engineering education not just answered my mechanical doubts but also fetched me a job with the development center of e-commerce giant Amazon. The professional stint gave me the realisation that my role in ensuring and enhancing customer satisfaction would not take me far merely based on bachelors credential. To live up to my expectations and the company’s aspirations, I need to append a master’s perspective to it.
